EcoXchange: Cultivating a Sustainable Future

About EcoXchange

EcoXchange is a pioneering digital platform founded by Sivanand C.K., connecting landowners and companies through verified ecosystem services. Our mission is to facilitate carbon sequestration, biodiversity restoration, and regenerative agriculture, creating a tangible impact on environmental health and local livelihoods.

Background: M.Sc aspirant in Ecosystem Services (Germany) from Wayanad, Kerala.

Ecosystem Restoration

Restore degraded forest land using native tree species, improving soil health and enhancing biodiversity.

Ecological Monitoring

Utilize satellite imagery, AI tools, and local field data to monitor ecological benefits like carbon stock and biodiversity count.

Verified Ecosystem Services

Package results as verified ecosystem service projects to attract CSR and green investment.

Technology & Impact

Technology Stack:

  • Remote Sensing: Sentinel, Landsat (NDVI, canopy cover)
  • Drone Surveys: Low-altitude for species counts, moisture checks
  • Field Methods: Mobile data collection, photographic biodiversity logs
  • AI Dashboards: For carbon stock and species analysis

Expected Outcomes:

  • CO2 Sequestration: ~4 tons per acre per year
  • Biodiversity Tracking: 20+ fauna species & 10+ flora indicators monitored
  • Soil Organic Carbon Improvement: +15% in 12 months (estimated)
  • Grant-Ready Reports and Replicable Model for other zones

Community & Social Engagement:

Local tribal youth and forest watchers will be involved in data gathering, tree care, and ecological monitoring. Honorarium/training opportunities will be provided, fostering local ownership and conservation interest.

Why This Matters:

  • Supports Kerala Forest Dept's climate targets and afforestation goals.
  • Attracts CSR and international green capital into local ecosystem restoration.
  • Builds a scalable model for youth-led, tech-driven forest credit generation.
  • Promotes sustainable livelihoods for local communities.
